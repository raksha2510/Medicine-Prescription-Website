ABDOMINAL PAIN

What is Abdominal Pain?


Abdominal pain is discomfort anywhere in your belly region between your ribs and your pelvis. We often think of abdominal pain as stomach ache, but pain in your abdomen could be coming from other organs too

What are the symptoms of Abdominal pain?
1.Severe pain
2.Fever
3.Bloody stools
4.Persistent nausea and vomiting
5.Weight loss
6.Skin that appears yellow
7.Severe tenderness when you touch your abdomen
8.Swelling of the abdomen

What causes Abdomial Pain?

Less serious causes of abdominal pain include:
1.Constipation
2.Irritable bowel syndrome
3.Food allergies
4.Lactose intolerance
5.Food poisoning
6.Stomach virus
7.Gas pain
8.Indigestion

What are the diagnosis and precautions to take at home?

Common causes of abdominal pain such as gas pain,indigestion will likely go away within a few hours to day,even without treatment.You can try over-the-counter(otc) medications for faster relief. Other causes of abdominal pain,such as food poisoning,gastritis,may go away after the stomach or intestinal lining has a chance to heal.

What are the prescribed medicines for Abdominal Pain?

1.Antibiotics for bacterial causes
2.Acid reducers and acid blockers
3.Pepto-bismol
4.Electrolyte replacement fluids for vomiting or diarrhea to prevent dehydration
