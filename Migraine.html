What is Migraine?


A migraine is much more than a bad headache that can cause severe throbbing pain or a pulsing sensation, usually on one side of the head.Migraine attacks can last for hours to days,and the pain can be so severe that it interes with your daily life.

What are the symptoms of Migraine?
Migraine have four stages:- prodrome,aura,attack and post-drome
1.Prodrome:-
Mood changes
Food cravings
Frequent yawning
2.Aura:-
Vision loss
Difficulty speaking
Weakness
3.Attack:-
Pain usually one side of your head
Nausea and vomiting
4.Post-drome:-
After a migraine attack,you might feel drained,confused and washed out for up to a day.

What causes Diabetes?

Stress at work or home can cause migraines.Strong smells such as perfume,paint thinner,smoke and others.Doctor’s also don’t know the exact cause of migraine,although they seem to be related to changes in your brain and to your genes.

What are the diagnosis and precautions to take at home?

There is no cure for migraine headache,but many drugs can treat or prevent them.So,for proper diagnosis for migraine , individuals must consult a doctor and take the required treatment.
