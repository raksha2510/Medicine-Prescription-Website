What is Meditreat?



Meditreat is a unique healthcare provider which deals with the betterment of health with an expert knowledge in the field of medications. The patients receive medicinal advices and the treatment at lifecare with thier convenience at their own places without any hustle of approaching to hospitals in case of minor symptoms.
Meditreat not only gives medicinal advices to the patients but also saves their lives by identifying the disease according to the symptoms being experienced by the individual.The patient is advised to seek doctor's advice in case of major health problem.
