ARTHRITIS

What is Arthritis?


Arthritis is a disease which affects our joints .Joints is the area where your bones meet and move. Arthritis usually involves pain,inflammation and breakdown of our joints. Mostly affected areas of our body by arthritis are feet,hands,hips,knees,lower back.

What are the symptoms of Arthritis?
1.Pain

2.Redness

3.Stiffness

4.Swelling

5.Tenderness

6.Warmth

What causes Arthritis?


1.Arthritis is a disease which keeps transferring from one generation to another generation.

2.Having a job or playing a sport which puts repeated stress on our joints is also a major cause of this disease.

3.Having certain autoimmune diseases or viral infections can also lead to arthritis.

What are the diagnosis and precautions to take at home?

Arthritis requires specific medication and can not be treated at home.So,for proper diagnosis for arthritis, individuals must consult a doctor and take the required treatment
